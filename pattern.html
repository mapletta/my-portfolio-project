Bruno and Bianca crochet pattern

Bruno & Bianca
Materials
  • Yarn: Brown, Red (Bruno); Light Gray, Pink (Bianca) Black for embroidery
  • Hook: 2,75mm (2mm recommended for some parts of the pattern)
  • Other:Poly Filling
  • Abbreviations: magic ring (mr), stitch (st), slip stitch (sl st), single crochet (sc), tr (treble crochet), dc (double crochet), front loop only (FLO)
  • Difficulty level: Beginner

    Legs & Body

  • First leg:
  • round 1: 10sc in MR (10)
  • round 2: (4sc, 1 inc) x 2 (12)
  • round 3: (5 sc 1 inc) x 2 (14)
  • round 4-5: 14sc (14)
  • round 6: (6sc, 1inc) x 2 (16), slip stitch into the next stitch and fasten off.
  • Second leg:
  • round 1: 10sc in MR (10)
  • round 2: (4sc, 1inc) x 2 (12)
  • round 3: (5sc 1inc) x 2 (14)
  • round 4-5: 14sc (14)
  • round 6: (6sc, 1inc) x 2 (16)
  • round 7: ch5 slip stitch into the 1st leg, sc in the same stitch 15sc, 5dc (in to the chains), 1sc into the 2nd leg + 15sc (42), slip stitch where the chain started
  • Body
  • round 8: 42sc (42) [ hint: starting with 5 sc in to the chain, 37 sc]
  • round 9-21: 42sc (42) ‚Äì 13 rounds
  • round 22: 14sc 1dec 19 sc 1dec 5sc (40) * the decreases might vary, make sure you dec at the sides of the bear
  • rounds 23-28: 40sc (40)
  • round 29: (3sc 1dec) x8 (32)
  • round 30: 32sc (32) Insert eyes, I used 8 mm between the rounds 24-25, around 8 rounds apart. Nose and muzzle: as per the picture, below the round n. 23 Start stuffing, stuff as you go.
  • round 31: (6sc 1dec) x 4 (28)
  • round 32: (5sc 1dec) x 4 (24)
  • round 33: (2sc 1dec) x 6 (18)
  • round 34: (1sc 1dec) x 6 (12)
  • round 35: 6 dec (6) Sl st into the next st and finish by cutting the yarn and pulling it out of the stitch.
  • Tail (I would recommend using a smaller hook, I used 2mm)
  • round 1: 4sc in MR (4)
  • round 2: (1sc 1inc) x 2 (6)
  • round 3: 6inc (12)
  • round 4: 12sc (12)
  • round 5: 1sc 1dec (8)
  • Sl st into the next st and finish by cutting the yarn and pulling it out of the stitch. Attached to the back of the bear, around 3 stitches above the leg space. The tail doesn‚Äôt need stuffing.
  • Arms (make 2)
  • round 1: 5sc in MR (5)
  • round 2: 5 inc (10)
  • round 3: 10 sc (10)
  • round 4: dec, 8 sc (9)
  • round 5: 9sc (9)
  • round 6: dec, 7 sc (8)
  • round 7: 8sc (8)
  • round 8: join both sides 3 sc, attach to the sides of the bear The arms don‚Äôt need stuffing.
  • Ears (make 2)
  • round 1: 6sc in MR (6)
  • round 2: 6inc (12)
  • round 3-4: 12sc (12)
  • Slip stitch into the next stitch and finish off. Fold the ears, they don‚Äôt need stuffing, but pay attention to shaping them to resemble these on the picture. Attach the ear(s) between rounds 29-32, align with the eyes like on the picture.
  • Heart (red yarn)
  • In MR ch 3, 2 tr, 2dc, 1 tr, 2dc, 2tr, 2ch and sl st into the MR; pull the yarn and tighten both yarn ends together at the back of the heart then attach the heart to the bear‚Äôs body at the height of the arm.
  • Rose (pink yarn, or any other colour you prefer)
  • round 1: ch 24
  • round 2: skip 1 ch, 4tr into the 2nd chain, sl st into the next ch, 4tr in the 4th ch, sl st into the next one, 4tr into the 6th ch, sl st into the next one, 4 tr into the 8th st, sl st into the next one, 4dc into the 10th st, sl st into the next one, 4dc into the 12th st, sl st into the next one, 4dc into the 14th st, sl st into the next one, 4dc into the 16th st, sl st into the next one, 4sc into the 18th st, sl st into the next one, 4sc into the 20th st, sl st into the next one, 4sc into the 22nd st, sl st into the next one, ch1
  • Put the petals together sew it together to shape it into a rose.

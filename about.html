About Me

I am Angelika

My interest for crocheting goes back to my childhood, when my beloved grandma taught me how to make a single crochet, however, it hasn't been until a year ago that I bought my first crochet hook and made my first amigurumi. Currently, I made amigurumis for my friends and family. I do my best as a pattern tester and I'm taking my first steps in pattern design. I'm glad coding allows me to combine my creative passions with a more technical mindset. 🐱‍👤🐱‍💻
